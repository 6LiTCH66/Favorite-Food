Turkish bread

For cooking you need:

  • Water
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Dry yeast
  • Sunflower oil

Cooking Instruction

Photos
  1. Dissolve yeast with sugar in warm water.
  2. Add salt, oil and flour to yeast water. Knead the dough. Leave up. During the ascent, knead twice.
  3. The dough rises very well.
  4. Divide the dough into four portions. Roll each into an oval and roll into a roll.
  5. Leave to rise for 20 minutes.
  6. Make an incision on the side of each bread.
    Then cover with the second baking sheet on top. First bake for 15 minutes at 250 ° C.
    Then remove the baking sheet and bake for another 20 minutes at 200 ° C.
  7. The bread turns out to be rosy. Cool a little and you can eat it.
  8. The top is crispy and the inside is tender crumb.
